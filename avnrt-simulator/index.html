Konsep Kunci

  • Typical: antegrade slow, retrograde fast → short RP, P buried/pseudo-S
  • Atypical: antegrade fast, retrograde slow → long RP, P negatif setelah QRS
  • Concealed: impuls SA tiba saat AVNRT aktif → block di proksimal (pathway refrakter)
